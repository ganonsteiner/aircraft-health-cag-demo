BOEING 737-800 NG
FLIGHT CREW OPERATIONS MANUAL — CHAPTER 2: LIMITATIONS
Applicable to: CFM56-7B powered 737-800 (Southwest Airlines fleet)

================================================================================
AIRSPEED LIMITATIONS (indicated airspeed unless noted)
================================================================================

VMO (Maximum Operating Speed):             340 KIAS / MMO 0.82
  Do not exceed in any phase of flight.

VFE (Maximum Flap Extended Speed):
  Flaps 1:   250 KIAS
  Flaps 2:   250 KIAS
  Flaps 5:   250 KIAS
  Flaps 10:  210 KIAS
  Flaps 15:  200 KIAS
  Flaps 25:  190 KIAS
  Flaps 30:  175 KIAS
  Flaps 40:  162 KIAS

VLO (Max Landing Gear Operating Speed):    270 KIAS (extension / retraction)
VLE (Max Landing Gear Extended Speed):     270 KIAS

Turbulent Air Penetration:                 280 KIAS / M0.76 (whichever is lower)
Windshield Wiper Operation:                250 KIAS maximum

================================================================================
POWERPLANT LIMITATIONS — CFM56-7B27E
================================================================================

Engine Model:         CFM International CFM56-7B27E
Rated Thrust (T/O):   27,300 lbf (sea level, static, ISA)
Rated Thrust (cont.): 24,900 lbf

Maximum EGT:
  Takeoff (5-min limit):     950 °C
  Maximum continuous:        925 °C
  Starting (transient):      725 °C

Oil Temperature:
  Normal operating range:    0 °C to 155 °C
  Maximum continuous:        155 °C

Oil Pressure:
  Minimum (idle):            13 psi
  Normal operating:          37–102 psi

N1 (Fan Speed):             100% = 5,188 rpm
N2 (Core Speed):            100% = 14,460 rpm
Maximum N1:                 104.0%
Maximum N2:                 105.0%

EGT Deviation Monitoring:
  Advisory threshold (above baseline):    +10 °C
  Maintenance inspection required:        +15 °C
  Ground aircraft pending borescope:      +20 °C

================================================================================
WEIGHT LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

Maximum Takeoff Weight (MTOW):   174,200 lbs (79,016 kg)
Maximum Landing Weight (MLW):    146,300 lbs (66,361 kg)
Maximum Zero Fuel Weight (MZFW): 138,300 lbs (62,731 kg)
Maximum Ramp Weight:             174,700 lbs (79,243 kg)
Operating Empty Weight (OEW):    ~91,300 lbs (41,413 kg) (configuration-dependent)

================================================================================
ALTITUDE LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

Maximum Operating Altitude:      41,000 ft (FL410)
Maximum Takeoff / Landing Alt:   8,400 ft pressure altitude (standard conditions)
Maximum Crosswind for T/O:       36 kts (demonstrated)
Maximum Demonstrated Crosswind:  38 kts

================================================================================
FUEL SYSTEM LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

Maximum Fuel Imbalance (wing tanks):   1,500 lbs
Total Fuel Capacity (usable):          ~46,100 lbs (6,875 US gal, typical config)

================================================================================
CREW REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

Minimum crew: 2 (Captain + First Officer)
Certificate: ATP required for PIC; Flight Engineer not required (EFIS-equipped)
